# Symmetric

## Overview

This directory develops the theory of symmetric polynomials in multivariate polynomial rings. It defines the key bases (elementary, complete homogeneous, power sum, monomial symmetric), proves the Fundamental Theorem of Symmetric Polynomials (that the symmetric subalgebra is freely generated by elementary symmetric polynomials), and establishes Newton's identities relating elementary symmetric polynomials to power sums. These results are foundational for algebraic combinatorics, representation theory, and Galois theory applications.
