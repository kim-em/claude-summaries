# GradedAlgebra

## Overview

The `GradedAlgebra/` directory provides the theory of graded rings and algebras, focusing on internally-graded structures where a ring or algebra decomposes as a direct sum of graded components. Core topics include basic graded algebra definitions and the canonical decomposition isomorphism; finite type results showing graded rings of finite type are generated by positive-degree homogeneous elements; homogeneous localization constructing subrings where numerators and denominators have the same grading; preservation of the Noetherian property by the degree-zero component; and homogeneous ideals with their prime and radical properties.
