## Overview

Reference library for MATH3325 Analysis 3 Honours course containing the complete Stein & Shakarchi Princeton Lectures in Analysis series (volumes 1-4) plus supplementary texts on functional analysis, Hilbert spaces, and spectral theory. Includes the required course textbook (Stein & Shakarchi Real Analysis Vol. III) with solutions manual, plus recommended references by Conway, Halmos, Helmberg, and Pedersen.
