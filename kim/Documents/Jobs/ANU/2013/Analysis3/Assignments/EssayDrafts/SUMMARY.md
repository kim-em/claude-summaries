# EssayDrafts

## Overview

Collection of 13 scanned draft essay submissions from MATH3325 Analysis 3 Honours students (October 2013), with extensive handwritten feedback from the instructor. Essays cover advanced analysis topics including Kakutani's and Brouwer's fixed point theorems, Nash equilibrium in game theory, Hausdorff measure, and fractal geometry. All files are scanned PDFs from a Konica Minolta SKMBT_C451 scanner, ranging from 743KB to 10.1MB and 2-9 pages each. Drafts show detailed instructor annotations including corrections, clarifications, structural suggestions, and mathematical feedback.
