# LectureNotes

## Overview

Comprehensive lecture notes (14 numbered lectures, 0-13) for MATH3325 Analysis 3 Honours course covering the progression from Hilbert space theory through measure theory to spectral theory and Banach spaces. Each lecture provided as both LaTeX source and compiled PDF. The sequence begins with motivating functional calculus as an infinite-dimensional generalization of eigenvalues/eigenvectors, develops Hilbert space theory (orthonormal bases, projections, adjoints), introduces compact operators and spectral theory, applies functional calculus to Fourier transforms and the Dirichlet problem, transitions to abstract measure and integration theory, and culminates with Radon-Nikodym derivatives, spectral theorems for self-adjoint operators, Banach space theory, and Fredholm theory. Also includes standalone proof of Stone-Weierstrass theorem (handwritten notes) and a flags.tex configuration file.
