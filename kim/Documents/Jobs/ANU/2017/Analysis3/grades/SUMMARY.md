# grades

## Overview

Grade records and calculations for MATH3325/MATH6214 Advanced Functional Analysis course from Second Semester 2017. Contains official grade roster reports showing final grades for 16 students (10 undergrads in MATH3325, 6 in MATH6214 including postgrad and research students), along with CSV exports of adjusted grades and a Mathematica notebook for visualizing grade distributions and applying logistic sigmoid transformations for grade scaling.
