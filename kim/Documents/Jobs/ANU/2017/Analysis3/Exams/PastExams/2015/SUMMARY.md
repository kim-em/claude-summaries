# 2015

## Overview

2015 take-home final exam (48-hour format) for MATH3325 Advanced Functional Analysis at ANU. The exam consists of 5 questions worth 20 marks each (with 10 bonus marks on Q5), covering Hilbert space convergence, Fourier multiplier operators on L^p spaces, weak-* topology on measure spaces, L^2/L^infinity norm inequalities in finite-dimensional subspaces, and characterization of weak convergence via convex hulls in reflexive Banach spaces. Full solutions are provided.
