# category-theory-2017

## Overview

MATH3349 Category Theory reading course materials from 2017 with 11 enrolled students. Course structured around Tom Leinster's "Basic Category Theory" textbook, following a student presentation and discussion format across weekly meetings. Students gave weekly presentations covering topics from basic definitions through Yoneda embedding, completed four assignments (40% total), wrote final essays (35%), and gave final presentations (15%). Course included special topics like David Spivak's categorical approach to databases and functorial data migration. Three weekly meetings: Wednesday presentations/discussion, Thursday assignment discussion, and Thursday Q&A/lecture with Scott.
