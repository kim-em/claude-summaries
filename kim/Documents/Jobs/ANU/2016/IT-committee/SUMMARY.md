# IT-committee

## Overview

IT committee work for MSI (Mathematical Sciences Institute) at ANU in 2016, focused on improving the departmental website management process. The committee proposed shifting from pre-publication to post-publication review of web edits, giving faculty direct editing access to relevant pages while maintaining administrative oversight through automated notification hooks in Drupal.
