# papers

## Overview

Collection of 7 selected research publications submitted with Dr. Saorín Gómez's ANU application, showcasing her contributions to convex geometry and Brunn-Minkowski theory. Publications span 2010-2015 and appear in high-quality journals including Journal of Geometric Analysis, Israel Journal of Mathematics, Monatshefte für Mathematik, and Forum Mathematicum. Papers demonstrate her expertise in quermassintegrals, inner parallel bodies, Steiner polynomials, mixed volumes, and polytope decomposition.
