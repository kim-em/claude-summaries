# papers

## Overview

Collection of 8 research publications by Sylvie Monniaux demonstrating her expertise in functional analysis, partial differential equations, and harmonic analysis. The papers span from 1997 to 2015 and cover topics including bounded imaginary powers of operators, maximal L^p regularity, heat kernels, Navier-Stokes equations in Lipschitz domains, the Stokes operator, singular integral operators on tent spaces, and the Lame system. Several papers are joint work with prominent collaborators including Matthias Hieber, Marius Mitrea, Pascal Auscher, Christoph Kriegler, Pierre Portal, and El Maati Ouhabaz.
