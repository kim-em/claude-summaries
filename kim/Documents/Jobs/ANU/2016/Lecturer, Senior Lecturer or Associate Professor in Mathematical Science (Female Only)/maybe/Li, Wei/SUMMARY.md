# Li, Wei

## Overview

Application materials for Wei Li, Associate Professor (tenured) at Institute of Theoretical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing. Her research focuses on string theory, mathematical physics, and holographic dualities, particularly higher-spin theory in AdS3 and its dual 2D conformal field theory. PhD from Harvard (2008, advisor Andrew Strominger) with postdoctoral positions at Kavli IPMU Tokyo, Max Planck Institute Potsdam, and Durham University. Recipient of Chinese Thousand Talents Plan (Young Professionals) and Max-Planck Partner Group Leader. Over 1300 citations on 20 publications.
