# papers

## Overview

Research publications by Dr. Arunima Ray in low-dimensional topology and knot theory. Contains two papers published in Algebraic & Geometric Topology (2015-2016) focusing on knot concordance, Casson towers, and satellite operators. These papers demonstrate expertise in the smooth/topological distinction in 4-manifold topology and connections between geometric constructions and algebraic filtrations of the knot concordance group.
