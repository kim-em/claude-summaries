# Reference_Books_and_Notes_Listed_in_Desprition_of_the_essay

## Overview

Collection of five reference materials on Brownian motion, stochastic calculus, and fractal geometry referenced in the functional analysis essay assignment. Contains four major textbooks totaling 36.6MB (327-454 pages each, all exceeding Claude's reading limits) covering stochastic calculus for fractional Brownian motion (Biagini et al.), fractal geometry (Falconer), diffusions and Markov processes (Rogers & Williams), and standard Brownian motion (Mörters & Peres), plus one concise 6-page set of course notes by Mark H.A. Davis (November 2004) providing a rigorous mathematical construction of Brownian motion using L2 theory, Haar functions, and the Lévy-Ciesielski construction method.
