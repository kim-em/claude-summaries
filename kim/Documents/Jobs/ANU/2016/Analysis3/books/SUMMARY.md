# books

## Overview

Reference textbook collection for MATH3325 Analysis 3 course. Contains the required text (Stein & Shakarchi Real Analysis Vol. III with solutions manual) and supplementary references covering functional analysis, Hilbert space theory, and spectral theory. Includes all four volumes of the Stein & Shakarchi Princeton Lectures in Analysis series plus additional specialized texts by Bressan, Conway, Halmos, Helmberg, and Pedersen. Two files use the DJVU format (Halmos and Helmberg).
