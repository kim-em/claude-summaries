This folder contains a single diagram PDF used in Lecture 11 (eigenvectors and eigenvalues). The diagram visualizes the definition of eigenvectors through a geometric example showing vectors and their transformations under matrix multiplication, illustrating that while vector u is an eigenvector (Au = -2u), vector v is not (Av ≠ λv for any scalar λ).
