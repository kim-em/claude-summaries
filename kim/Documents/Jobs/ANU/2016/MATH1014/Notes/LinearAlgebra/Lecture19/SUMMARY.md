# Lecture19

## Overview

Inner products and orthogonality in Euclidean spaces (Lay §6.1-6.2). Introduces dot products, vector length, unit vectors, orthogonal vectors, orthogonal complements, and the fundamental relationship between row/column spaces and null spaces. Motivates least squares solutions for overdetermined linear systems. Includes the Basis Theorem showing that bases for W and W⊥ combine to form a basis for ℝⁿ, and decomposition of vectors into components in W and W⊥.
