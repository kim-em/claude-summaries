# Lecture09

## Overview

Lecture 9 materials covering dimension theory in linear algebra. Topics include finite-dimensional vector spaces, dimension as the number of vectors in a basis, dimensions of subspaces, the Basis Theorem, rank and nullity of matrices, the Rank Theorem, and the row space of a matrix. Includes applications to Hermite polynomials from quantum mechanics and theoretical examples relating rank to solvability of linear systems.
