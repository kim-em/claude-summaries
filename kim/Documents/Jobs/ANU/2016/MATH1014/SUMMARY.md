# MATH1014

## Overview

MATH1014 (Mathematics and Applications 1) Semester 2 2016 teaching materials for a large first-year calculus and linear algebra course with approximately 400 students, co-taught with Griffith Ware. Contains comprehensive course materials including weekly worksheets, assignments, mid-semester and final exams (separate calculus and linear algebra components), grade management spreadsheets tracking preliminary and final grades, SELT (Student Experience of Learning and Teaching) evaluation materials, tutorial group assignments, and email contact lists for students and tutors.
