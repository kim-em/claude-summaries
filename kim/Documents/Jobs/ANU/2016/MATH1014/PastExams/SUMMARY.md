# PastExams

## Overview

Collection of past examination papers from 2014-2015 for the MATH1014 linear algebra component, provided to students for exam preparation and practice. Includes both mid-semester and final examinations with complete sample solutions, covering topics such as lines and planes in 3D, linear transformations, basis and coordinate systems, subspaces, and null/column spaces. These materials demonstrate the exam format and difficulty level students could expect in the 2016 course.
