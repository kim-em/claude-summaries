# O.Donnol, Danielle

## Overview

Application materials for Danielle O'Donnol, who applied for the 2016 ANU female-only continuing position in Mathematical Science. O'Donnol was a Visiting Assistant Professor at Indiana University with a PhD from UCLA (2008) under advisors Erica Flapan and Robert Brown. Her research focused on knot theory, spatial graph theory, Legendrian graphs, Floer homology, and DNA topology. She had an NSF research grant ($144,577, 2014-2017), extensive teaching experience including as Calculus I Coordinator at Oklahoma State University, and notable laboratory experience in molecular biology from her undergraduate years at Indiana University. Required visa sponsorship.
