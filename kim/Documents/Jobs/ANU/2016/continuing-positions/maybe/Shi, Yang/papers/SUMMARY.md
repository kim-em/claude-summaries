# papers

## Overview

Selected research publications by Yang Shi demonstrating her expertise in integrable systems and discrete Painlevé equations. These papers showcase breakthrough work on geometric reductions connecting multi-dimensional ABS lattice equations to discrete Painlevé systems, establishing fundamental connections between discrete integrable systems and Weyl group symmetries through novel geometric approaches.
