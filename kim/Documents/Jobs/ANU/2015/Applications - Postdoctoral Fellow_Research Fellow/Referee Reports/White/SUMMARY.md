# White

## Overview

Collection of 6 reference letters supporting Noah White's application for the ANU postdoctoral fellowship position #507048. The letters are from distinguished mathematicians across multiple institutions (University of Sydney, University of Edinburgh, Northeastern University) and provide exceptionally strong recommendations for White's candidacy.
