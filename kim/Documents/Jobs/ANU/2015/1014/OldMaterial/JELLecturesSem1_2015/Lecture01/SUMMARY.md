# Lecture01

## Overview

First lecture materials for MATH1014 (Mathematics and Applications 2 - Algebra) covering course introduction and foundational content on coordinates, vectors, and geometry in ℝ³. The directory contains LaTeX source files for both course administrative information and mathematical content, compiled Beamer presentation PDFs (both screen and print versions), and extensive Graffle diagrams with exported PDFs illustrating geometric concepts. The lecture introduces the course structure, textbooks (Stewart's Essential Calculus and Lay's Linear Algebra), assessment methods (including WebAssign quizzes and tutorial participation), and mathematical content covering coordinate systems, surfaces in 3D space, vector definitions, vector operations (addition and scalar multiplication), and worked examples involving velocity vectors and geometric applications.
