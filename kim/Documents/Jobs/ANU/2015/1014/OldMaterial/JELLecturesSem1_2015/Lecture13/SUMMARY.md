# Lecture13

## Overview

Lecture 15 materials covering the characteristic equation and eigenvalue computation (Lay §5.2). This lecture builds on the previous introduction to eigenvalues/eigenvectors by teaching methods to find eigenvalues via the characteristic polynomial det(A - λI) = 0. Topics include computing characteristic equations for matrices of various sizes (2×2, 3×3, 5×5), the concept of algebraic multiplicity, matrix similarity and similarity transformations, and detailed applications to dynamical systems and Markov chains with population migration examples. The lecture includes both Beamer presentation slides and Maple computational worksheets demonstrating eigenvalue calculations.
